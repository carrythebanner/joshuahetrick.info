About

I use design to solve problems for people. Technology is a tool to get there, but people are always at the center.

I love public spaces like parks, plazas, and libraries. A successful software project looks a lot like a great public space:

  • has a clear purpose, but is adaptable;
  • is planned for the future, while giving its past due consideration;
  • and of course, is accessible for everyone.
